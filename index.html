01. General Protocol

Common Sense

Not every violation is written. If you are being a nuisance or disrupting the community flow, staff will intervene regardless of specific rules.

Staff Authority

Staff decisions are final. Arguments in public channels result in an immediate mute. Appeals must be sent via Ticket with evidence.

Alt Accounting

Using secondary accounts to bypass mutes or bans will result in a permanent IP-ban from our infrastructure.

02. Severe Violations

Malicious Activity (Beaming)

Any attempt to compromise accounts via phishing, QR-logging, or suspicious links is an instant blacklist. We do not tolerate "beaming" here.

Hate Speech & Slurs

Zero tolerance for racial, homophobic, or transphobic slurs. Bypassing filters to post these will lead to a permanent ban without warning.

Doxxing & Threats

Leaking private information or making credible threats towards members or staff will lead to legal reporting and permanent removal.

NSFW Content

This includes baits, profile pictures, and stickers. Keep the hub professional. We are a scripting community, not a dump.
